## About me

I am a full-stack developer based in Dehradun, India, focused on building
reliable products with thoughtful user experiences. I work across React,
Next.js, React Native, Expo, Node.js, Django, and the infrastructure needed to
ship them.

- Building education products at **Goreeva** across mobile, web, and backend systems
- Strong focus on mobile architecture, real-time features, data-heavy interfaces, and product UX
- Comfortable owning a product from the first screen through production releases
- B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering, Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology
